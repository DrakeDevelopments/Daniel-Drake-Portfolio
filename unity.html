Introduction

A 2D action adventure game, with some souls-like mechanics. I've given it the codename of "The Tower". The aim is to break out of the tower by overcoming numerous battles and puzzles. You start off in a tutorial area, where you learn to run, jump, and attack. Defeating ememies rewards you with a currency that's used to purchase level-ups. There are also abilities that can been purchased and equipped to power you up even further! The equipable abilities are currently in development, however, the leveling up system is functional.

Upon completing the tutorial you gain access to a "home" area, where you can shop, level up, buy a house, and more! There are multiple areas each with their own themes, bosses, and plot lines. All previous bosses must be defeated in order to challenge the final boss. At which point the end-credits roll and you can continue exploring the map and completing side quests.
